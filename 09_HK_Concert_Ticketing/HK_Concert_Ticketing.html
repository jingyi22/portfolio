5 Core UX Issues

Across 4 platforms and 8 concerts, we identified 5 UX and security gaps in Trip.com's current concert ticketing experience.

Policy Notices Hard to Find

Ticket rules hidden in popups; "cannot modify after order" notice not prominent enough, Users miss critical info at key moments.

Weak Info Confirmation

Lacks dedicated confirmation page with no secondary review mechanism. Errors cannot be detected before submission, leading to entry rejection complaints.

Incomplete Disclaimer

Missing clear disclaimers on user error and ticket transfer risks, Users unclear about their responsibilities, Frequent disputes.

Weak Order Management

Ticket codes fully exposed with no screenshot protection — ticket fraud risk is high with no accountability.

Guest Purchase Risk

Trip.com is the only platform allowing guest checkout — no account binding means no purchase history, harder identity verification, and higher fraud exposure.
